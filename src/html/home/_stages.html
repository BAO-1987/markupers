stages of website creation

Creating a site is a very painstaking work of every specialist, because if you remove at least one stage - wait for the crash

  1. Design

    This is one of the most important stages of development, because it is from these steps that your profits and brand recognition depend

    Hours: 50-500

  2. Coding

    Is the phase where the website creation is started.This phase of web development involves a lot of time and effort to finalize a website

    Hours: 100-1000+

  3. Testing

    Removing every bug and deliver and quality software. Identification of errors, fixing glitches and bugs, testing for validation

    All the time of project development (300-1000+ h)
